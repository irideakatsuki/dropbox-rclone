Dropbox rclone Config Generator

Enter your Dropbox app credentials to generate an rclone config file.

How to get your credentials:
1. Go to the Dropbox App Console .
2. Create a new app (Full Dropbox or App folder access).
3. Add {{ callback_url }} to the Redirect URIs list in your app settings.
4. Copy the App key (Client ID) and App secret (Client Secret) here.
